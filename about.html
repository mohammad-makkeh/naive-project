About Naive

Naïve is an art space in Badaro, Lebanon, where creativity is encouraged and nurtured. It is open to all, regardless of experience or background, fostering a community of passionate individuals. Naïve offers a variety of art activities, workshops, and events to stimulate creativity and imagination. The café provides a respite for artists and art lovers, offering a cozy atmosphere to enjoy a cup of coffee or a treat. The space is a hub for creative expression and a place to connect with others.

Meet The Owner

Emné, the founder of Naïve, is a Lebanese artist and art therapist who believes in the transformative power of art. She sees it as a tool for healing, connection, and community building. Emné's passion for art is infectious, and her dedication to fostering a supportive and inclusive environment is evident in every aspect of the art space. She embraces the diversity of perspectives and artistic expressions that enrich the Naïve community.

Our Mission

Naïve's mission is to promote art therapy and creativity. They believe that art can be used to heal and connect people. They offer a variety of art classes, workshops, and events to help people of all ages explore their creativity. Naïve also creates a general atmosphere that is conducive to socializing. The art space is bright and inviting, and the staff is always happy to strike up a conversation. Naïve's commitment to creating a welcoming and inclusive environment makes it a great place to meet new people and make friends.
